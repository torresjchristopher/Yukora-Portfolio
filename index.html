What is Data Invisibility?

Data Invisibility means that to us, you don't exist. Yukora software is built to be a "Ghost in the Machine." We do not use telemetry, we do not track usage patterns, and we do not have a "backdoor" to your activities. Our software functions entirely on your local hardware, making your digital life invisible to third-party observers.

The Principle of Data Ownership

We believe that if it's on your drive, it belongs to you. Yukora software never claims ownership of the scripts you write, the data you process, or the results you generate.
